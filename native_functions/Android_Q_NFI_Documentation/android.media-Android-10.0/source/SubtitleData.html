 {@link android.media.MediaPlayer.TrackInfo} of the subtitle track, one of
 {@link android.media.MediaFormat#MIMETYPE_TEXT_CEA_608}, {@link android.media.MediaFormat#MIMETYPE_TEXT_CEA_708},
