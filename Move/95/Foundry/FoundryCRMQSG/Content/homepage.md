 

QuickStart Guide Series - Building the VoltMX Foundry CRM Sample App (Connecting to Line-of-Business)

This document provides details of the VoltMX Foundry business connector to easily incorporate Salesforce data in a mobile app built using the VoltMX Foundry SDK for PhoneGap.
